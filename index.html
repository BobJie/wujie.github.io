    • Recent Projects

  • Robust Belif Rule Bases for medical data analysis

    • Given the interpretable of Belif Rule Bases(BRB) model, BRB model has been applied in many fields, such as medical analysis, industrial control. However, it is very difficult to build an effective BRB model, since a good parameter settings should be set in advance. Usually, the experts are required to give the initial values of the parameters. Obviously, this kind of initialization is of higher variation. Thus, we coorperate with Prof. Zhou on the data-inspired initialization of the parameter settings for BRB model on medical data analysis. Moreover, given the complexity of BRB model, some effective ensamble strategies are also studied in this project.

  • Iterative fusion for unsupervised SAR image change detection

    • Due to the conherence imaging method of Synthtic Apture Radar (SAR) system, random speckle is always accompanied with SAR images. This also gives a large influence on the SAR change detection. In this project, we do some researchs on the designation of iterative fusion scheme to improve the change detection performance, especially for the property analysis of different samples at different stages.

  • Stochastic distances for unsupervised SAR image processing

    • Due to the conherence imaging method of Synthtic Apture Radar (SAR) system, random speckle is always accompanied with SAR images. This also gives a large influence on the SAR image processing. In this project, we do some researchs on the statistical modeling of SAR images and stochastic distances with the build stistical distributions, such as K-L distance, Reyi distance and Hellinger distance. Also, some entropy based method will be studied in this project. The main application about the denosing and change detection of SAR images.
